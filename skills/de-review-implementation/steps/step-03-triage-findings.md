# Step 03 - Triage Findings

Turn candidate issues into an actionable report.

1. Order by severity: blocker, high, medium, low.
2. Keep only findings with behavioral, contractual, operational, security, cost, or maintainability impact.
3. Merge duplicates.
4. Convert weak findings into open questions or residual risks.
5. Check that artifact compliance gaps are not hidden as low severity when they block acceptance criteria.
