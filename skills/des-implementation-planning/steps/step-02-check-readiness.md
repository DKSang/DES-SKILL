# Step 02 - Check Readiness

Decide whether implementation can start.

Check for:

- Approved scope or change brief.
- Clear acceptance criteria.
- Data contract, DQ, lineage, security, and operational requirements when relevant.
- Test data, validation commands, and expected evidence.
- Migration, rollback, or backfill constraints.

HALT when any blocker would force the developer to guess requirements. Route the blocker to the exact artifact or owner.
