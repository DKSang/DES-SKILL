# Step 02 - Analyze Outcomes

Compare planned and actual delivery.

Assess:

- Scope completed, partial, or dropped.
- Artifact drift between DES docs and implementation.
- Technical debt introduced or retired.
- Data quality, lineage, security, observability, cost, and performance outcomes.
- Process gaps that slowed or confused implementation.

Blockers stay blockers. Do not re-label them as lessons learned.
