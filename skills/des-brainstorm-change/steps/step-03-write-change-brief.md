# Step 03 - Write Change Brief

Write `{output_file}` as the single handoff artifact.

Required structure:

- Status and date.
- Change summary.
- Affected artifacts and consumers.
- Decision log.
- Risks, assumptions, blockers, and open questions.
- Recommended next workflow.

The recommendation must be one of: proceed to `des-implementation-planning`, revise a specific DES planning artifact, gather missing input, or stop.
