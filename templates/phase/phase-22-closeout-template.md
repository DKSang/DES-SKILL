# Phase 22 Final Closeout

## 1. Purpose

This closeout records the final workflow decision after Project Evaluation.

It tells the project team whether the workflow is complete, complete with risks, ready for implementation handoff, ready for MVP/demo, ready for internal review, blocked, or should route into a next iteration.

## 12. Final Do-Not-Assume List

Do not assume:

* design completion means production readiness;
* missing evidence means success;
* stakeholder acceptance exists without feedback;
* adoption exists without usage or acceptance evidence;
* quality readiness exists without DQ results or explicit design-only limitation;
* release readiness exists without CI/CD and release evidence;
* risks disappear because the workflow closed.
