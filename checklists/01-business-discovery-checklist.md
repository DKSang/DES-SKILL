# Checklist - Business Discovery

## Required Context

- [ ] Project intent is defined.
- [ ] Business problem is stated in non-technical language.
- [ ] Primary consumer or stakeholder group is identified.
- [ ] At least one target decision or use case is identified.
- [ ] Expected value is documented.
- [ ] MVP or first-release scope is defined or explicitly marked as unresolved.
- [ ] Non-scope is documented.
- [ ] Constraints are listed.
- [ ] Initial success criteria are listed.
- [ ] Open questions are captured.

## Guardrails

- [ ] The artifact does not jump into schema design.
- [ ] The artifact does not prescribe ingestion implementation.
- [ ] The artifact does not define final KPI formulas.
- [ ] The artifact does not design dashboard, API, semantic model, ML model, or app implementation.
- [ ] Technology choices are treated as constraints or preferences, not business goals.
- [ ] Assumptions are explicitly marked.
- [ ] No missing business decision is silently invented.

## HALT Validation

- [ ] Project intent HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Primary consumer HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Target decision/use case HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Expected value HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] MVP scope HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Success criteria HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.

## Result

Choose one:

- [ ] Passed
- [ ] Passed with risks
- [ ] Blocked
