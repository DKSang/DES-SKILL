# Checklist - Requirements and KPIs

## Required Context

- [ ] Phase 1 Business Discovery artifact exists or Draft continuation is explicitly accepted.
- [ ] Phase 2 Business Question Catalog exists or Draft continuation is explicitly accepted.
- [ ] P1/P2 business questions are identified.
- [ ] Consumers are identified.
- [ ] Scope and non-scope are considered.

## Functional Requirements

- [ ] Each P1 business question maps to at least one functional requirement.
- [ ] Each P1 functional requirement has a consumer.
- [ ] Each P1 functional requirement is testable or reviewable.
- [ ] Each P1 functional requirement has priority and status.
- [ ] Requirements do not include implementation design prematurely.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- [ ] Freshness expectations are documented where relevant.
- [ ] Reliability expectations are documented where relevant.
- [ ] Quality expectations are documented where relevant.
- [ ] Security/privacy constraints are documented where relevant.
- [ ] Cost/performance constraints are documented where relevant.
- [ ] Usability/discoverability needs are documented where relevant.
- [ ] Governance/ownership needs are documented where relevant.

## KPI / Metric Quality

- [ ] Each approved KPI has business meaning.
- [ ] Each approved KPI maps to a business question or success criterion.
- [ ] Each approved KPI has a formula or clear definition.
- [ ] Each approved KPI has a grain.
- [ ] Each approved KPI has an owner or approver.
- [ ] Each P1 KPI has acceptance criteria.
- [ ] Candidate metrics are clearly marked as Candidate or Draft.
- [ ] Conflicting metrics are marked as Conflicting and not silently resolved.

## Guardrails

- [ ] The catalog does not design Gold tables.
- [ ] The catalog does not choose final data sources.
- [ ] The catalog does not design pipelines or transformations.
- [ ] The catalog does not design dashboards, semantic models, APIs, or ML models.
- [ ] The catalog does not include implementation code.
- [ ] Assumptions are explicitly marked.
- [ ] Open questions are documented.

## HALT Validation

- [ ] Missing upstream question HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] KPI formula HALT resolved or KPI remains Candidate/Draft.
- [ ] Metric grain HALT resolved or KPI remains Candidate/Draft.
- [ ] Metric owner HALT resolved or KPI remains Candidate/Draft.
- [ ] Freshness/SLA HALT resolved or requirement remains Draft.
- [ ] Metric conflict HALT resolved or metric remains Conflicting.
- [ ] Acceptance criteria HALT resolved or requirement remains Draft.

## Result

Choose one:

- [ ] Passed
- [ ] Passed with risks
- [ ] Blocked
