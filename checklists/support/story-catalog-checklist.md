# Checklist — Story Catalog

## Required Context

- [ ] Epic Catalog exists or Draft continuation is explicitly accepted.
- [ ] Selected epics are identified.
- [ ] DES artifact coverage is checked.
- [ ] Story creation scope is defined.
- [ ] Existing Story Catalog is checked if updating.

## Story Coverage

- [ ] Story design principles are defined.
- [ ] Story generation rules are defined.
- [ ] Story inventory is created.
- [ ] Every story has a story ID.
- [ ] Every story has a title.
- [ ] Every story maps to an epic.
- [ ] Every story has a story type.
- [ ] Every story has priority.
- [ ] Every story has status.
- [ ] Every P1 epic has at least one story or documented blocker.
- [ ] Foundation stories exist where needed.
- [ ] Ingestion/Bronze/Silver/Gold stories exist where relevant.
- [ ] Contract/quality stories exist where relevant.
- [ ] Orchestration/observability stories exist where relevant.
- [ ] Semantic/serving stories exist where relevant.
- [ ] Governance/security stories exist where relevant.
- [ ] CI/CD/release stories exist where relevant.

## Story Quality

- [ ] Every story has clear goal and benefit.
- [ ] Every story has included scope.
- [ ] Every story has excluded scope.
- [ ] Every story has acceptance criteria.
- [ ] Every story has test expectations.
- [ ] Quality expectations are included or marked not applicable.
- [ ] Governance/security expectations are included or marked not applicable.
- [ ] Dependencies are documented.
- [ ] Risks/blockers are documented.
- [ ] Story-to-epic mapping is documented.
- [ ] Story-to-DES artifact mapping is documented.
- [ ] Story dependency map is documented.
- [ ] Definition of Ready is included.
- [ ] Definition of Done is included.

## Guardrails

- [ ] Story Catalog does not contain implementation code.
- [ ] Story Catalog does not contain sprint plan.
- [ ] Story Catalog does not contain detailed task breakdown.
- [ ] Stories are not untraceable guesses.
- [ ] Stories are not too broad as entire epics.
- [ ] Stories are not too small as single-line tasks.
- [ ] Missing context is marked Draft/Risk/Blocked.
- [ ] Sprint planning is deferred to `des-sprint-planning`.

## HALT Validation

- [ ] Epic Catalog availability HALT resolved or catalog remains Draft.
- [ ] Story creation scope HALT resolved or catalog remains Draft.
- [ ] Epic selection HALT resolved or selected stories remain Draft.
- [ ] Story granularity HALT resolved or story remains Draft.
- [ ] Acceptance criteria source HALT resolved or story remains Draft.
- [ ] Test expectation source HALT resolved or story remains Draft.
- [ ] Missing critical context HALT resolved or story remains Draft/Blocked.
- [ ] Sprint-level request HALT resolved if applicable.

## Result

Choose one:

- [ ] Passed
- [ ] Passed with risks
- [ ] Blocked
