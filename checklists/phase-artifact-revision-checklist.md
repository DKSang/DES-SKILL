# Phase Artifact Revision Checklist

Use this checklist to verify that the phase artifact has been revised based on evidence.

## Inputs

- [ ] Phase artifact exists.
- [ ] Phase support plan exists.
- [ ] Evidence pack exists or evidence waiver is documented.
- [ ] Evidence gaps are known.
- [ ] Critical findings are known.

## Revision Traceability

- [ ] Each major revision references evidence.
- [ ] Unsupported assumptions are removed or marked.
- [ ] Contradicted assumptions are corrected or removed.
- [ ] Confirmed assumptions are kept with evidence.
- [ ] Open questions remain visible.

## Decision Updates

- [ ] Decisions are updated based on evidence.
- [ ] Decision rationale is documented.
- [ ] Reversibility is documented when relevant.
- [ ] Draft decisions are not presented as final.
- [ ] Open decisions are marked clearly.

## Risk Updates

- [ ] Risks are updated based on evidence.
- [ ] Reduced risks are marked.
- [ ] Increased risks are marked.
- [ ] Carry-forward risks are marked.
- [ ] Risk mitigation is documented when possible.

## Artifact Quality

- [ ] Artifact reflects the latest known facts.
- [ ] Artifact separates facts, assumptions, risks, and open questions.
- [ ] Artifact is usable by the next phase.
- [ ] Artifact does not hide missing evidence.
- [ ] Artifact does not overstate confidence.

## Revision Report

- [ ] Revision summary is written.
- [ ] Section-by-section revision log is filled.
- [ ] Items not revised are listed.
- [ ] Revision result is selected.
- [ ] Next action is recommended.

## Completion

- [ ] Revised artifact is ready for Done Gate, or blockers are documented.
