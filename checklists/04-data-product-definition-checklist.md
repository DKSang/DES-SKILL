# Checklist - Data Product Definition

## Required Context

- [ ] Phase 1 Business Discovery artifact exists or Draft continuation is explicitly accepted.
- [ ] Phase 2 Business Question Catalog exists or Draft continuation is explicitly accepted.
- [ ] Phase 3 Requirements and KPI Catalog exists or Draft continuation is explicitly accepted.
- [ ] Primary consumers are identified.
- [ ] P1/P2 use cases or business questions are identified.
- [ ] Requirements and success criteria are considered.

## Product Definition Quality

- [ ] Data product name and intent are defined.
- [ ] Primary consumer is defined.
- [ ] Secondary consumers are listed where relevant.
- [ ] Supported use cases are listed.
- [ ] Product outputs are defined.
- [ ] First release output is clearly marked.
- [ ] Product boundary is clear.
- [ ] Non-scope is documented.
- [ ] Product lifecycle status is defined.
- [ ] Product owner or steward is defined.
- [ ] Success criteria are mapped to requirements or KPIs.

## Service and Trust Expectations

- [ ] Freshness/SLA expectation is documented for P1 outputs.
- [ ] Reliability expectation is documented where relevant.
- [ ] Quality/trust expectation is documented.
- [ ] Access/serving expectation is documented.
- [ ] Support or escalation expectation is documented where relevant.
- [ ] Known limitations are documented.
- [ ] Contract expectation is identified for P1 outputs.

## Governance and Risk

- [ ] Security and access considerations are captured.
- [ ] Sensitive data or privacy considerations are captured where relevant.
- [ ] Dependencies are listed.
- [ ] Risks are listed.
- [ ] Assumptions are explicitly marked.
- [ ] Open questions are documented.

## Guardrails

- [ ] The artifact does not design physical schemas.
- [ ] The artifact does not design source ingestion.
- [ ] The artifact does not design transformations.
- [ ] The artifact does not create full data contracts.
- [ ] The artifact does not design semantic model internals.
- [ ] The artifact does not design dashboards, APIs, or application implementation in detail.
- [ ] The artifact does not include code.

## HALT Validation

- [ ] Missing upstream requirements HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Primary consumer HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Product boundary HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] First release output HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Service expectation HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Product owner HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Lifecycle status HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.
- [ ] Trust expectation HALT resolved or artifact remains Draft.

## Result

Choose one:

- [ ] Passed
- [ ] Passed with risks
- [ ] Blocked
